Leonard Nimoy

as Spock

1931 - 2015

Leonard Nemoy was an actor, director, author, singer a,d photographer. His most wel know role that of Spock in the Star trek franchise. The character is a half-vulcan half-human start fleet officer. Together with James Kirk and the crew of the USS Enterprise, they explore the far reaches of space. Leonard portrayed Spock from 1964, in Star Trek: The Original Series to 2013 in the recent reboot film, Star Trek Into the Darkness.
